EcoEmpire

Sustainable Power Plant Tycoon

EcoEmpire is a sustainable twist on a classic venture-capitalist tycoon game. It considers factors like fair wages, worker satisfaction, and environmental impact while still understanding the nuance that "saving the world" is not easy and takes funding and diligence. Navigate the complex world of energy and try to generate profits while maintaining sustainable environmental practices and working conditions.

Don't let pollution reach 1000! Don't let worker satisfaction drop below 50! Don't let customer satisfaction drop below 50! Don't go in debt! These issues will hurt profit! If you are over 5000 dollars in debt, you will not be able to go into more debt and purchase anything.

The "generate" button passes one day in time. Win the game by reducing pollution to net zero.

Bonus challenge: try to win the game in less than a year, or see how you can mininize the time it takes to reach zero pollution.
